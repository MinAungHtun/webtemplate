The Holy Island of LindisFarne

Withs its holy background and castles dating back to medieval ages,
it is surely a treasure of Christianity. Experience the feel of peace
and tranquility on this very enchanted island. We are blessed to
have a chance to build our dance studio in this holy and peaceful
environment. With added beauty of the natural scences, there could be
no better place in the world to discover your inner-you through dancing.

This island accommodates about 650,000 visitors every year.
People of all ages and career backgrounds come for the very
mysteriousness and holyness of this island.
If you are a tourist, make sure that you have a place to rest
as we have a very limited places available in our domitory.
Please check the accommodations.Check Accommodations.
If the residences are full,don't worry. You can stay at nearest towns.
For locals, make sure that you arrive in time as you don't
want to be missing the fun.
