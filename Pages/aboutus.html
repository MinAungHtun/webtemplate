AboutUs

Founded by College Drop-outs

We were electrical engineering students before we
found our passion in ballroom dance.
We dropped out from MIT(Massachusetts Institute of Technology)
and yes we are indeed that passionate about dance.
We've also successfully created that passion around our studio
too.Sort of miss the student life there too.
Still confuse about your dreams?

History

We chose Lindisfarne Island as our paradise destination for
its grace and history. LDFBDC was first founded in 2016.
Since then, the membership has risen dramatically from time to time
for the island's might and our hardwork.

The club has been modified from time to time and we have reached
quite of a dance community. The club's location makes it easier
for its worldwide reputation and now we firmly established
a successful ballroom dance studio for all the dancaholics like us.
