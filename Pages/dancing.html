Dancing

Dance

Dance is an ancient art passed on and evolved from generation
to generation.It is recognized as an art
capable of inspiring imaginations and entertaining people.
Dance has many types that derived from various ethic groups
and background each with their own power of inspiration and beauty.

Philosophy

Dance reflects civilization and civilization in turns, reflects dance.
Almost every racial tribes use dance as an expression of their inner emotions.
Dancing is an art where physical and emotions combines.It is where people
get to experience true freedom of creativity.People dance to express and
to compose their feelings and inner-soul through their body movements.

From the imperialistic movements of Ballroom dance to the swifty feet
of Cha Cha and from the elegance of expressive Cultural dances to
the free flowing explosive Street-dance, dancing has it all.
Contributing a significant part in people of all social classes.

Ballroom dance

LINDISFARNE ballroom dancing club happens to be a school
that continues the legacy of former ballroom dancing legends.
With its elegance and tenderness,ballroom dance happens to be
a dance of aristocrats and a show of high class.
