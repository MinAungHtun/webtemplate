Uniform

Men's Uniforms

A competition class dress-up can cost from $80 to $2000 for an apparel.
Different types of apparel makes up a ballroom dance suit. Apparels can range
from different types such as shoes,shirt,pants,vest,tail suit,necktie,bowtie,collars,
cufflinks, shirt stuffs and handkerchief. The quality and combinations
may vary depending on your choice of dance and competition.

Women's Uniform

Women often wears dresses up to $6,000 depending on
fabrics and competition. Women wear athletic underpants
under their dresses. High heels are wore
in replacement of shoes.

Athletic Wears

LDFBDC offers and recommends members athletic clothes for
their flexibility and agility. It will make you feel more comfortable
than a competition suit. No casual suits will be used when
training unless of club's events.We will use versatile clothings
(sports bra, sportwears) while training.
